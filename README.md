# Trans Siberian Railway Planner
This page has the schedule of two popular lines of the Trans Siberian Railway from Moscow to Vladivostok (Vladivostok to Moscow to be added later). It covers the 002Э, or "Rossiya" train, and also the unnamed 010H train. Both run from Moscow to Vladivostok daily and take 7-8 days if you stayed on the train the whole time.

## Why did you build it?
There is not a good website that allows users to just browse all the stops on the Trans Siberian line and understand:
- How much time does it take to get between any two stops (ie How much time will I be stuck on a train?)
- What does the train timing look like? (ie Will I depart or arrive in the middle of the night?)
- What are the local arrival and departure times from stations? (in Russia, train times are quoted in Moscow time even though the country spans many time zones)
- What other stops may I want to consider to break up a longer journey?

In considering a rail trip through Russia, this site is designed to allow users to see the distance and timing between any two stops, so that they can better plan their trans siberian trips. It also provides a quick overview of the most popular tourst stops along the length of the rail line, so that users can get an idea of where they might like to stop on their journey.

## Considerations
The site uses static timetable data found on the Tutu website. It is hard coded and not dynamically updated the the latest schedules. So it's current as of June 26, 2026. If the timing of the schedules changes in the future, it won't be automatically updated.

The RZD website supposedly has an API that you can query, but this was not meant to be a dynamically updating website.
